MO MAGGI

Ingredients:

  • 1 packet of Maggi noodles
  • 1 cup thick curd (yogurt)
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 small tomato, finely chopped
  • 1 small green chili, finely chopped (adjust to your spice preference)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to your spice preference)
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk the curd (yogurt) until it's smooth and creamy. Add the cooked Maggi noodles to the curd and mix well. Let it marinate for a while to absorb the flavors.
  2. In a pan, heat 2-3 tablespoons of oil. Add cumin seeds and mustard seeds. When they start to splutter, add a pinch of asafoetida (hing).
  3. Add the finely chopped onions, green chilies, and ginger-garlic paste. Saut√© until the onions turn golden brown.
  4. Add the finely chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and mushy.
  5. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well and cook for a couple of minutes until the oil separates.
  6. Remove the pan from the heat and let the mixture cool slightly.
  7. Once the mixture has cooled down, add it to the marinated Maggi noodles and curd. Mix everything well to combine the flavors.
  8. Garnish with freshly chopped coriander leaves.
  9. You can also serve curd Maggi with a wedge of lemon for some extra tangy flavor (optional).
Your Curd Maggi is ready to be served! It's a creamy, tangy, and flavorful dish that's quick and easy to make. Enjoy!
