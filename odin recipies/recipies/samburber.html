Sambar Burger Recipe

Ingredients:

For the Sambar:

  • 1 cup toor dal (split pigeon peas)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 small tomato, chopped
  • 1 small carrot, chopped
  • 1 small potato, chopped
  • 1/4 cup green beans, chopped
  • 1/4 cup drumsticks, chopped (optional)
  • 1/4 cup tamarind pulp
  • 1 tsp sambar powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1-2 tbsp oil

For the Burger:

  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 veggie burger patties (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tomato, thinly sliced
  • Lettuce leaves
  • 4 cheese slices (optional)
  • 4 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 4 tbsp ketchup
  • Butter for toasting the buns

Instructions:

Prepare the Sambar:

  1. Wash the toor dal and pressure cook it with 2 cups of water until it's soft and mushy. Set aside.
  2. In a large pot, heat the oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and saut√© until they turn translucent.
  3. Add the tomatoes, carrot, potato, green beans, and drumsticks (if using). Cook for a few minutes until they start to soften.
  4. Add the tamarind pulp, sambar powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Pour in the cooked toor dal and bring the mixture to a boil. Simmer for about 10-15 minutes or until the vegetables are tender and the sambar is well-flavored.

Assemble the Burgers:

  1. While the sambar is simmering, heat a grill or a skillet and cook the veggie burger patties according to the package instructions until they're nicely browned.
  2. Split the burger buns in half and lightly toast them with a little butter in the same skillet or on the grill.
  3. Mix the mayonnaise and ketchup to make a sauce.

Assemble the Sambar Burgers:

  1. Place a lettuce leaf on the bottom half of each burger bun.
  2. Add a veggie burger patty on top of the lettuce.
  3. Spoon a generous portion of the sambar over the patty.
  4. Place a cheese slice on top of the sambar (if using).
  5. Add a few slices of onions and tomatoes.
  6. Spread the mayonnaise-ketchup sauce on the top half of the buns and place it on top of the burger.

Enjoy your unique and flavorful Sambar Burger!
